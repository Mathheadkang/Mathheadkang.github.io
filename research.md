# Research Interest
Broadly speaking, my research interest focuses on understanding special metrics and their moduli spaces on manifolds, motivated by physics and topology. These metrics include locally conformally flat metrics, Yamabe metrics, non-KÃ¤hler Hermitian metrics, and hyperbolic type metrics.
